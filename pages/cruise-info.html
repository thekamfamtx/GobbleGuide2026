🚗 Parking & Arrival

Terminal:
Erato Street Cruise Terminal

Address:
1100 Port of New Orleans Place
New Orleans, LA 70130

Carnival Cruise Line terminal located on the New Orleans riverfront with easy access from I-10 and close to the French Quarter.

Parking:
Erato Street Parking Garage

Covered and secured parking with over 1,000 spaces available.

Garage Opens:
9:00 AM CST

Vehicle Restrictions:
Maximum height: 8' 4" (limited third floor spaces)
7' 6" on higher floors
Maximum length: 22 feet

Parking Cost:
$200 for 8 days without reservations
Reservations add a $1 convenience fee per day

💡 Tip:
Arrive early to allow time for parking, luggage drop-off, and check-in.
